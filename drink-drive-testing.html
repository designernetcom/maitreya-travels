Drink and Drive Testing

  • Overview
  • Gallery

At Maitreya Travels, our commitment to passenger safety extends to stringent measures regarding drink and drive testing. We conduct daily tests for our drivers, maintaining a zero-tolerance policy for alcohol consumption while on duty. This rigorous approach not only ensures compliance but also offers passengers the peace of mind that their safety is our foremost priority. Our consistent testing regimen reflects our unwavering dedication to providing a secure and responsible travel experience for all.

Features Of Drink and Drive testing

  • Daily Driver Testing: Each day, our supervisors conduct tests to prevent any issues related to alcohol consumption among our drivers.
  • Zero-Tolerance Policy: We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy towards alcohol consumption while on duty, ensuring passengers' safety is never compromised.
  • Passenger Peace of Mind: Our regular testing regimen offers passengers the assurance that incidents involving alcohol-related driving are completely averted.
